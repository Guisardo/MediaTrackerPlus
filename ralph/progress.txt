- useFacets and useMultiValueSearchParam are in `client/src/hooks/facets.ts` and `client/src/hooks/useMultiValueSearchParam.ts`
- Facet setters remove 'page' from params to reset pagination; they do NOT touch orderBy/sortOrder (preserved via spread)
